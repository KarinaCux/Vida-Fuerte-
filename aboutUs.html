About Us


Vida Fuerte! is dedicated to providing comprehensive health resources and services to our community. Our mission is to empower individuals to take control of their health through education, support, and access to quality care. We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to live a healthy and fulfilling life. OUR FOCUS: MAINTAIN, ACCESS, AND ADVOCATE FOR HEALTH. Many communities of color suffer so much health concerns due to socioeconomic and socitial contstraints. Even now, with federal cuts affecting families, we are here to keep families not only stay stable, but advocate for thier health.
